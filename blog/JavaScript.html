March 24, 2016

This week we dipped our feet in the JavaScript pool. It was a bit jarring when transfering from Ruby but after getting over the initial shock of it there are many similarities. One of which is hashes in Ruby are quite similar to JavaScrips objects as they both contain keys and values that are accessed by those keys but there are a few differences that are important to keep in mind.
