This week was a deeper dive into Ruby methods and classes. One of the main types of methods we are imploying are enumerables. So now comes the obvious question, what the heck is an enumarable? Well they are methods associated with sorting classes of objects that have an each method available to them (arrays and hashes). These methods simplify some common tasks such as sorting and finding information within. In this post I will review one such method in detail.
